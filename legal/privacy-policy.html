InterviewPro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

InterviewPro is a professional interview management application published by Arkadiusz Kawalko ("Data Controller", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data in connection with your use of the InterviewPro application.

2. Data We Collect

InterviewPro is a local-first application. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's built-in storage (IndexedDB / localStorage). We do not operate any backend servers and do not transmit your data to our systems.

Data that may be stored locally on your device includes:

  • Candidate names, contact details, and employment history
  • Interview notes, scores, and assessments
  • Recruiter/interviewer names and settings
  • Custom industries and questions you create
  • Calendar events and reminders

3. How We Use Your Data

Data stored in InterviewPro is used solely to provide the interview management functionality within the application. We do not access, process, sell, or share your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The application loads font assets from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This request is subject to Google's own privacy policy. No candidate or interview data is transmitted to Google.

The optional WhatsApp and SMS sharing features, if used, open the respective messaging applications on your device with pre-filled text. No data is sent to our servers. The recipient's handling of that message is governed by WhatsApp's and your mobile carrier's respective policies.

5. GDPR and UK GDPR Rights

If you use InterviewPro to process the personal data of candidates, you act as the Data Controller for that data. You are responsible for ensuring that:

  • Candidates are informed about how their data will be used
  • Appropriate consent is obtained where required
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary
  • Candidate requests for access, rectification, or deletion are honoured

InterviewPro includes a built-in GDPR consent form to assist with these obligations.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all application data at any time by using the Backup & Settings → Clear All Data option within the app, or by clearing your browser's site data for InterviewPro.

7. Children's Privacy

InterviewPro is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always available within the application and at this URL. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
